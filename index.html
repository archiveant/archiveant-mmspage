Frequently Asked Questions

What is Military Essentials Bot Services?

Military Essentials Bot Services (MEBS) is a professional Discord bot hosting platform that provides reliable, secure, and high-performance infrastructure for Discord bots and automation systems.

How do I get started with hosting?

To get started, contact us through our official Discord server or support channels. Our team will guide you through the setup process, including bot submission, credential configuration, and deployment.

What types of bots can you host?

We host a wide variety of Discord bots including:

  • Moderation and administration bots
  • Music and entertainment bots
  • Utility and productivity bots
  • Gaming and community bots
  • Custom automation systems

All bots must comply with Discord's Terms of Service and Developer Policies.

What is your uptime guarantee?

We strive for 99.9% uptime with our enterprise-grade infrastructure. While we cannot guarantee 100% uptime due to factors like scheduled maintenance, hardware failures, or third-party outages, we make every effort to minimize downtime and communicate any issues promptly.

How secure are my bot credentials?

Security is our top priority. We implement:

  • AES-256 encryption for credential storage
  • TLS/SSL encrypted transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Restricted personnel access with audit logging
  • Isolated hosting environments per client
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide technical support for hosting-related issues including deployment problems, performance optimization, uptime monitoring, and infrastructure concerns. However, we do not provide bot development or coding support.

Can I access my bot's logs and metrics?

Yes, you have access to your bot's operational logs, error reports, performance metrics, and uptime statistics through our monitoring systems.

What happens if my bot goes down?

Our automated monitoring systems detect downtime immediately and trigger automatic restart procedures. If the issue persists, our team is notified and will investigate and resolve the problem as quickly as possible.

How do I terminate my hosting service?

You can terminate your hosting service at any time by contacting our support team. Upon termination:

  • Your credentials will be securely deleted within 30 days
  • Hosting data will be retained for 7 days for recovery purposes
  • You should revoke or regenerate your bot tokens
  • Final backups can be provided upon request

Do you offer database hosting?

Yes, we can provide database hosting solutions including PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Redis as part of our hosting services. Contact us to discuss your specific database requirements.

What programming languages do you support?

We support bots written in any language that can run on our infrastructure, including:

  • Python (discord.py, nextcord, py-cord)
  • JavaScript/TypeScript (discord.js)
  • Java (JDA)
  • C# (.NET)
  • Go (discordgo)
  • And many others

Is there a limit on bot size or resource usage?

Resource limits depend on your hosting plan. We offer scalable solutions to accommodate bots of all sizes, from small community bots to large-scale applications serving thousands of servers. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
